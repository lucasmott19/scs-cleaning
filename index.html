About the S.C.S Committee

The S.C.S committee was co-founded and founded by Hanna, Marilena, Lucas, and Christian when we were in 6th grade. We made the committee to clean up our school environment both indoors and outdoors. We offer cleanings for Teachers' classrooms during our free periods, email us to find out when we are free. Thank you for all your support. !
